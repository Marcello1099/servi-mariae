**ServiMariae** — Latin for *Servants of Mary* — is a small Catholic blog
gathering reflections on faith, theology, and the everyday practice of
Christian love. The name points to Mary as our model and intercessor, and
to the work of every disciple who seeks to serve Christ through her.

Posts here draw on Scripture, the Church Fathers, the saints, and
contemporary Catholic writers. The aim is not novelty but clarity: to
read the deposit of faith with the mind of the Church, and to live it.

> *Beati mundo corde, quoniam ipsi Deum videbunt.* — Mt 5:8
